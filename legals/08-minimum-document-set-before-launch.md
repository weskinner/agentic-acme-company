# 08. Minimum Document Set Before Launch

## Purpose

Defines the minimum legal packet required to launch while managing core legal and operational risk.

## Minimum Viable Legal Packet

## Entity and Governance
- Filed Articles of Organization
- EIN confirmation
- Signed Operating Agreement

## Confidentiality and IP
- One-way NDA template
- Mutual NDA template
- Contractor IP assignment template

## Commercial and Customer Policies
- POD vendor agreement reviewed and accepted
- Website Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Shipping policy
- Return/refund policy

## Optional but Strongly Recommended Before Launch
- Preliminary trademark search memo
- Influencer/affiliate agreement template
- Data retention and access policy

## Pre-Launch Readiness Checklist
- All templates stored in a central legal folder.
- Final signed copies clearly labeled and versioned.
- Counterparty legal names verified.
- Authorized signatory confirmed for all contracts.
- Governing law and dispute terms reviewed.
- High-risk clauses highlighted for counsel.

## Minimum If Partner Joins Pre-Launch
- Member admission consent
- Amended and Restated Operating Agreement
- Equity purchase or grant agreement
- Capital contribution terms
- Vesting/repurchase terms (if applicable)

## Stop-Launch Conditions
Pause launch until fixed if any are true:
- No signed operating agreement.
- No clear IP ownership from contractors.
- No customer-facing terms/privacy/return policy.
- No tax registration where legally required.
