# 07. Recommended Sequence (Practical Order)

## Purpose

Provides the order of operations to reduce legal risk and rework while launching an LLC-based apparel brand with POD fulfillment.

## Phase 1: Entity Setup (Week 1)
1. Choose formation state and confirm naming availability.
2. File Articles of Organization.
3. Obtain EIN.
4. Appoint registered agent.

Deliverables:
- Entity formed and documented
- EIN issued

## Phase 2: Internal Governance (Week 1-2)
1. Execute initial Operating Agreement.
2. Execute initial written consent/resolutions.
3. Set up compliance calendar.

Deliverables:
- Signed governance documents
- Role and authority clarity

## Phase 3: Tax, Banking, and Licensing (Week 2-3)
1. Open business bank account.
2. Register sales tax and other required accounts.
3. Apply for local/state licenses and permits.

Deliverables:
- Bank and tax setup complete
- Operational permissions in place

## Phase 4: IP and Confidentiality Controls (Week 2-4)
1. Start trademark clearance process.
2. Put NDA templates in place.
3. Implement contractor IP assignment templates.

Deliverables:
- IP ownership protection underway
- Controlled disclosure process established

## Phase 5: Commercial Contract Stack (Week 3-5)
1. Review and negotiate POD vendor terms.
2. Publish website legal policies.
3. Finalize influencer/affiliate agreement templates.

Deliverables:
- Customer and vendor legal posture prepared for launch

## Phase 6: Partner Admission (As Needed)
1. Agree partner economics via term sheet.
2. Prepare partner admission and equity docs.
3. Execute amended operating agreement and member consent.
4. Update cap table and records.

Deliverables:
- Partner admitted with clear rights and obligations

## Phase Gates (Do Not Skip)
- Do not share sensitive financial/vendor data without NDA.
- Do not grant equity without updated operating agreement language.
- Do not launch storefront without customer-facing legal policies.

## Suggested 30-Day Milestone
By day 30, target completion of:
- LLC formation and EIN
- Base operating agreement
- Tax and bank setup
- NDA and IP assignment templates
- POD agreement review
- Website Terms/Privacy/Returns policies
