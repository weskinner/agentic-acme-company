# 04. IP and Brand Protection Documents

## Purpose

Protects ownership of brand identity and creative assets for an apparel and print-on-demand business.

## IP Assets to Inventory
- Brand name(s)
- Logos and marks
- Taglines/slogans
- Product artwork and graphics
- Product photography/video
- Domain names and social handles
- Website copy and design assets

## Trademark Documents

## 1) Trademark Search Records
- Document preliminary and full searches before filing.
- Capture:
  - Exact mark search
  - Similar/confusingly similar marks
  - Relevant classes (apparel and related goods)

## 2) Trademark Filing Package
- Application draft
- Specimen strategy (use in commerce vs intent to use)
- Filing receipts and office action responses
- Renewal calendar and maintenance deadlines

## Copyright and Creative Ownership
- Keep source files and creation records for original artwork.
- Use written agreements for all creators.
- Track publication/use dates when relevant.

## Work-for-Hire and Assignment Documents
- Require signed IP assignment from:
  - Freelance designers
  - Agencies
  - Photographers/videographers
  - Contractors creating marketing assets
- Include warranties of originality and non-infringement.

## Brand Governance Documents
- Brand style guide (logo use, colors, typography, placement).
- Trademark usage guidelines for partners/affiliates.
- Approval workflow for external use of brand assets.

## Infringement Response Toolkit
- Monitoring SOP (marketplaces, social platforms, web).
- Cease-and-desist templates.
- DMCA takedown process and evidence checklist.
- Escalation matrix (informal outreach, counsel letter, platform complaint).

## POD-Specific IP Risk Controls
- Verify design originality before publication.
- Avoid copyrighted characters, slogans, or close derivatives.
- Keep a design provenance record:
  - Creator
  - Date
  - Source files
  - Licenses used (fonts, stock assets)

## Red Flags
- No signed IP assignment from contractors.
- Launching without trademark screening.
- Reusing internet graphics without verified license.
- Missing evidence files for takedown disputes.
