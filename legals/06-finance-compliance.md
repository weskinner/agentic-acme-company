# 06. Finance and Compliance Documents

## Purpose

Covers the legal and operational documents needed to support banking, taxes, bookkeeping, and audit readiness.

## Banking and Entity Verification
- Stamped formation documents
- EIN confirmation letter
- Operating Agreement
- Member/manager resolution authorizing bank account signers
- Beneficial ownership information (as required)

## Tax Registration and Filings
- Federal tax classification election documentation
- State tax registrations (sales/use tax, employer taxes if applicable)
- Resale certificate (where applicable)
- Local tax account registrations

## Accounting Governance Documents
- Accounting policies memo (cash vs accrual, revenue recognition approach)
- Chart of accounts setup
- Expense reimbursement policy
- Approval matrix for spending thresholds

## Contractor and Payroll Compliance
- Contractor intake package:
  - W-9 (US)
  - Independent contractor agreement
  - Confidentiality/IP assignment clauses
- 1099 reporting process and deadlines (US)
- Employee onboarding package if/when hiring begins

## Record Retention Policy
- Define retention periods by category:
  - Contracts and legal documents
  - Tax records and filings
  - Bank statements and reconciliations
  - Payroll and contractor records
- Define storage location and access permissions.

## Internal Compliance Calendar
- Monthly close and reconciliation deadlines
- Sales tax filing deadlines
- Annual federal/state filing dates
- State annual report/franchise tax due dates

## Risk Areas to Monitor
- Commingling personal and business funds
- Incomplete documentation for deductible expenses
- Missing resale certificate requirements
- Late tax registrations in nexus states

## Finance Compliance Checklist
- Open business bank account and restrict personal use.
- Set up bookkeeping system and chart of accounts.
- Register for required tax accounts.
- Implement monthly close process.
- Archive all signed contracts and material invoices.
