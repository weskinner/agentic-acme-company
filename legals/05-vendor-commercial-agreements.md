# 05. Vendor and Commercial Agreements

## Purpose

Defines legal terms that govern print vendors, ecommerce operations, customer policies, and external commercial partnerships.

## Vendor Agreement Coverage (POD/Fulfillment)

## 1) Service Levels and Operations
- Production time SLA
- Shipping method and estimated delivery windows
- Tracking requirements
- Peak-season capacity language

## 2) Quality and Defect Handling
- Print quality standards and tolerances
- Reprint/refund responsibility allocation
- Inspection and dispute windows
- Chargeback and claims workflow

## 3) Pricing and Payment Terms
- Unit costs and shipping costs
- Surcharge and price-change notice periods
- Payment timing and late fee terms

## 4) Liability, Indemnity, and Risk Allocation
- IP infringement indemnity scope
- Limitation of liability caps
- Exclusions of consequential damages
- Insurance requirements (if any)

## 5) Termination and Transition
- Convenience termination rights
- Cause-based termination triggers
- Data/export and transition support to new vendor

## Customer-Facing Policies (Website)
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Shipping policy
- Return/refund policy
- Terms for promotional offers and discounts

## Influencer and Affiliate Agreements
- Deliverables and content usage rights
- Approval rights and disclosure compliance (FTC)
- Compensation model (flat fee, affiliate %, hybrid)
- Exclusivity and non-disparagement terms
- Performance reporting and payment schedule

## Wholesale/Retailer Agreement Basics
- Purchase terms and reorder minimums
- Territory/channel restrictions
- MAP/pricing policy (if used)
- Returns, markdowns, and damaged goods handling
- Brand presentation and merchandising standards

## Checklist Before Signing Any Vendor Contract
- Verify legal entity names match exactly.
- Confirm governing law and dispute venue.
- Confirm termination and cure rights.
- Confirm ownership and access rights to operational data.
- Confirm SLA and quality remedies are explicit.
