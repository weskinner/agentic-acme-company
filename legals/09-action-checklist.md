# 09. Action Checklist

## Purpose

A practical execution checklist to move from planning to signed documents and compliant launch.

## Week 1
- Select formation state.
- File LLC (Articles of Organization).
- Obtain EIN.
- Draft initial Operating Agreement.
- Appoint registered agent.

## Week 2
- Open business bank account.
- Register tax accounts and required business licenses.
- Build legal file system (drafts, finals, expirations, renewals).
- Prepare NDA templates (mutual + one-way).

## Week 3
- Prepare contractor agreement with confidentiality and IP assignment.
- Start trademark clearance and mark shortlist.
- Review POD vendor terms and identify redlines.
- Draft website Terms, Privacy, Shipping, Returns policies.

## Week 4
- Finalize and execute core agreements.
- If adding partner: execute admission package and amended operating agreement.
- Update cap table and ownership records.
- Build annual compliance calendar.

## 60-Day Follow-Through
- Complete trademark filing strategy.
- Implement NDA log and contract renewal tracker.
- Validate tax filings and nexus footprint.
- Review any high-risk clauses with counsel.

## Ownership Matrix (Fill In)
- Founder:
  - Owns filing process, signing authority setup, and bank/tax onboarding.
- Legal counsel:
  - Reviews partner/equity docs and key commercial agreements.
- CPA/bookkeeper:
  - Confirms tax registrations, filing deadlines, and books setup.
- Operations lead:
  - Manages vendor agreements and policy implementation.

## Status Tracker Template
Use this table for each legal document:

| Document | Owner | Drafted | Reviewed | Signed | Renewal Date | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Example: Operating Agreement | Founder | Yes | Yes | Yes | N/A | Final v2 |

## Final Launch Sign-Off
- Core legal packet complete.
- Partner paperwork complete (if applicable).
- Customer-facing policies published.
- Compliance dates entered into reminders.
- Counsel review complete for high-risk items.
