# 02. Ownership and Partner Onboarding Documents

## Purpose

This file covers legal documents and negotiation points when admitting a new LLC partner (member), whether by purchase, grant, or phased vesting.

## Partner Admission Paths

## 1) Buy-In (Partner Purchases Interest)
- Typical document: Membership Interest Purchase Agreement.
- Defines:
  - Purchase price
  - Percentage interest transferred/issued
  - Payment terms
  - Representations and warranties
  - Closing conditions

## 2) Earn-In or Grant (Equity for Services/Performance)
- Typical documents:
  - Equity grant/award agreement
  - Vesting and repurchase terms
- Define:
  - Vesting start date and schedule
  - Cliff and acceleration conditions
  - Termination outcomes (good leaver/bad leaver)

## 3) Capital Contribution-Based Entry
- Typical documents:
  - Capital contribution agreement
  - Updated capital account schedule
- Define:
  - Cash/assets/services contribution
  - Timing and milestones
  - Consequences for missed contributions

## Required Document Package

## 1) Amended and Restated Operating Agreement
- Must update:
  - Ownership table/cap table
  - Voting rights
  - Profit/loss allocations
  - Manager roles and decision authority
  - Transfer restrictions and buy-sell terms

## 2) Member Written Consent
- Approves partner admission and terms.
- Should reference exact attached agreements.

## 3) IP Contribution/Assignment Agreement
- Required when partner contributes brand assets, designs, domain names, or social accounts.

## 4) Buy-Sell and Dispute Clauses
- Address:
  - Deadlock resolution
  - Death/disability
  - Voluntary exit
  - Forced removal triggers
  - Valuation mechanics

## Key Negotiation Terms to Document Clearly
- Equity percentage now vs fully diluted assumptions.
- Voting rights vs economic rights.
- Salary/guaranteed payments and distributions.
- Time commitment and role responsibilities.
- Non-compete/non-solicit enforceability scope (state-specific).

## Data Intake for Drafting
- Current cap table and operating agreement version.
- New partner legal name/address/entity details.
- Agreed role and expected weekly contribution.
- Equity economics and vesting terms.
- Governance rights (board/manager vote rights).

## Red Flags
- Admitting partner without amending operating agreement.
- Undefined vesting/repurchase mechanics.
- No clear deadlock mechanism.
- No documented IP ownership transfer.

## Closing Checklist
- Final negotiated term sheet signed.
- All agreements signed and dated by all required parties.
- Cap table updated.
- Banking and tax records updated if needed.
- Internal ownership ledger archived.
