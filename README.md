# Agentic Acme Company

This repository contains planning and operations documents for launching a print-on-demand t-shirt business with social media-driven growth.

## Project Contents

- [tshirt-dropshipping-business-plan.md](tshirt-dropshipping-business-plan.md): Full business plan for the t-shirt POD model.
- [legals/legal-documents-overview.md](legals/legal-documents-overview.md): Master overview of legal requirements and document planning.
- [vendors/vendors-overview.md](vendors/vendors-overview.md): Operational setup guide for printing and shipping vendors (for example Printify-style workflows).

## Suggested Usage Order

1. Start with [tshirt-dropshipping-business-plan.md](tshirt-dropshipping-business-plan.md).
2. Review [legals/legal-documents-overview.md](legals/legal-documents-overview.md) and complete the legal checklist.
3. Implement vendor operations using [vendors/vendors-overview.md](vendors/vendors-overview.md).
4. Keep legal, vendor, and financial documents updated as decisions are finalized.

## Important Disclaimer

These documents are business planning resources and not legal, tax, or accounting advice. Use qualified legal and financial professionals to review final decisions and agreements.
